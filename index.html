Interior Design Studio

Interno Della Casa is a multi-disciplinary design studio and creative consultancy based in Milan. Founded by creative director Tonnito Muramelly in 1996, the studio specializes in interior architecture, hospitality design, art direction, and branding.

An award-winning international interiors studio rooted in the principles of serene and ergonomic design, we specialize in luxurious residential, commercial and public spaces, underpinned by a contemporary European aesthetic.

700+ project all over the world

Since 1996, we’ve been creating imaginative spaces all over the world, from urban apartments to independent retail boutiques. We also run a creative product design practice.
